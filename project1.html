Overview

This website represents all the projects I have completed in Year 11 Digital Technology. You will also see my design considerations, development and implications. You'll see my progression throughout the creation of the project. Character: This character is roughly 47 years old, he was born in Ghana, West Africa. The character's name is Kojo Abeeku. He actively works as Senior Partner at B&P Assosciates. Kojo enjoys playing Basketball in his free time, he is very skilled in cooking, as he grew up in a poor household where he was needed every night to cook food. Kojo is married to his wife, Afafa Abeeku, and his 2 children are Adzo Abeeku, and Amma Abeeku. Kojo suffered from Anorexia but is currently recovering.

    Implications

    Aesthetics

    Aethetics is necessary for a character as it will make the character look appealing. This is important in a low-poly character as without enough aethetics, it will look plain and boring. If the character looks good, this will encourage more users to play with the character.

    How I will address this:

  • I will ensure that my character has aesthetics by making blue coloured shorts, a red shirt and some shades. I will get feedback on this design too.
  • I will also make sure that despite it being a low-poly character, there are no unfinished, plain parts on the character.

    • Functionality

    The functionality of a low-poly character is simply about if the character works or not. Primarily the rigging, for example, do the arms move? Do the legs work properly?

    How I will address this:

  • I will ensure the character is functioning as intended by testing the animations, double and even triple checking the rigging, and getting help where needed.

  • Usability

    The usability is ensuring that the character moves smoothly. This is important for the low-poly character as if it did not work, the character would no longer be useful.

    How I will address this:

  • I will ensure that the character runs smoothly and as inteded.
  • I will also make it easy for the developers that use this character by labelling each part correctly so they can identify it.
